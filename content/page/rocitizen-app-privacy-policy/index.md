# Privacy Policy for RoCitizen

**Last Updated**: December 2, 2025

## Overview

RoCitizen ("the App") is a Romanian citizenship quiz application designed to help users study for Romanian citizenship tests. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user information.

## Information Collection and Use

RoCitizen does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data. All quiz questions and answers are stored locally within the app and no data leaves your device.

Specifically:

- We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
- We do not track your quiz scores or progress
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools
- We do not share any information with third parties
- We do not require user accounts or registration

## Data Storage

All app functionality operates entirely on your device. Quiz questions, images, and app preferences are stored locally and are never transmitted to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
